### 3) Advantages

# 2.Namesilo ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### 1) Direct access to official website: 
<a href="https://www.namesilo.com/" title="Namesilo Official Website">Click here to enter</a>
![Namesilo homepage](/img/2/2-3.avif)

### 2) Advantages
1. No real-name authentication required

2. Supports a wide range of domain name types (.com, .net, .org, .top, .xyz, etc.)

3. Free WHOIS protection

4. Supports Alipay payment
### 4) Disadvantages
1. Some domain names are more expensive (.com, .net)

2. Does not support some country-specific suffixes (.kg, .nz, .mk, etc.)

# 3.Alibaba Cloud ⭐⭐

### 1) Alibaba Cloud Official Website: 
<a href="https://wanwang.aliyun.com/domain" title="Alibaba Cloud Official Website">Click here to enter</a>

![Alibaba Cloud homepage](/img/2/2-4.avif)

### 2) Advantages
1. Free WHOIS protection (except for .cn domain names)

2. Supports Alipay payment

3. Supports many .cn suffix domain names (.com.cn, .net.cn, etc.)

4. Supports Chinese suffix domain names (.wangluo, etc.)

### 4) Disadvantages
1. Domain names are relatively expensive (.com, .net)

2. Does not support some country-specific suffixes (.kg, .nz, .mk, etc.)

3. Real-name authentication required
