Until this year, I completely said goodbye to the trouble of renewing memberships - because I found an amazing music streaming app that is **open-source, free, lightweight, and rich in music sources**: MusicFree.

## 2. Configure Music Sources

After opening the app, click on **Plugin Management** in the sidebar, then select **Subscription Settings**; subsequently click **Add** to add a subscription source, fill in a custom name for the source, enter `https://musicfreepluginshub.2020818.xyz/plugins.json` in the link field, and click **Save** and update the subscription after completion. After the operation, you will find a large number of new subscription sources in the plugin library.

## 3. Import Music Playlists

This step is simple: copy the link of your playlist, paste it into the corresponding plugin, and import it.

## 4. Cloud Synchronization

Open **Jianguoyun** and register/log in [Official Website Direct Link](https://www.jianguoyun.com/d/signup)

Click on **Account - Security Options - Third-Party Application Management** to create an application password

Return to MusicFree

Click on **Settings** in the upper right corner, click on **Backup and Restore**, and find **Webdav** Backup

Fill in the following information:

- URL: `https://dav.jianguoyun.com/dav/`
- Account: Your `email` or `mobile phone number` used to register Jianguoyun
- Password: The `application password` you just obtained

Click **Backup Playlist** to complete cloud synchronization.
